Ska Radios of the world, unite!

When I started File Under Ska in 2003, livestreams were rare, but became increasingly prevalent. Timezones (with daylight saving time and half-hour steps) are really tricky to implement. To add your show, of if you find errors, send your details to matthias@file-under-ska.de. If you are a nerd geek like me, create a merge request for https://github.com/mhaeusser/ska.one/blob/main/skaradios.html.
