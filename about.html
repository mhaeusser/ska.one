Ska Radios of the World, Unite!

About

When I started File Under Ska in 2003, it was only available via FM radio in Stuttgart - a city known for its challenging topography.
In the years that followed, the Freies Radio für Stuttgart and other independent stations began offering internet livestreams, reaching listeners all over the world.
Around that time that, I started building a simple HTML page listing the shows I knew, including their airtimes and timezones, but other projects got in the way.
I've had this idea for a long time and secured the domain when it became available.

Timezones (with daylight saving time and half-hour offsets) are really tricky to implement. AI helped with the JavaScript code, but there is no warranty for correctness...
