Ska Radios of the World, Unite!

About

When I started File Under Ska in 2003, people could only hear it via FM in Stuttgart (which is renowned for its challenging topography).
In the following years, the Freies Radio für Stuttgart and other free radios came up with internet livestreams, reaching listeners all over the world.
It was at that time that I started programming an HTML page with an overview of the radio shows I knew, including their show times and timezones, but other projects came in the way.
I've had this idea for a long time and reserved the domain when it came out.

This is one of the projects that AI helped finish. Timezones (with daylight saving time and half-hour steps) are really tricky to implement. So there is no warranty for correctness...
